# Privacy Policy

Effective date: [2026-04-27]  
Last updated: [2026-04-27]

[bcds] we provides a data analysis service that helps users analyze trends from their own authorized TikTok account.

---

## 1. What data we collect

After you explicitly authorize via TikTok OAuth, we may access and process:

- Basic account identifiers: `open_id`, display name (from TikTok `user.info`)
- Video metadata from your authorized account: `id`, `title`, `video_description`, `share_url`, `create_time`
- Video performance metrics: `view_count`, `like_count`, `comment_count`, `share_count`
- Technical logs for reliability and troubleshooting (request timestamps, error codes)

We do **not** access private messages, passwords, or data outside the scopes granted by TikTok.

## 2. How we use data

We use the data only to:

- collect trend candidates from your authorized TikTok account
- rank/filter videos (e.g. by view thresholds)
- generate analytics outputs and reports for your use
- maintain service stability and debug failures

## 3. Legal basis and authorization

We process TikTok data based on your explicit authorization through TikTok OAuth and the scopes you grant (currently: `user.info.basic`, `video.list`).

## 4. Data retention

We retain collected TikTok data for up to [90 days] unless a longer period is required by law or explicitly requested by you.  
After retention expires, data is deleted or anonymized.

## 5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data.  
We may share data only with essential service providers (e.g. hosting, database, monitoring) under confidentiality and security obligations.

## 6. International transfer

If data is processed outside your country/region, we apply reasonable safeguards required by applicable laws.

## 7. Your rights

You may request to:

- access your data
- correct inaccurate data
- delete your data
- withdraw authorization (disconnect TikTok)

To exercise these rights, contact: [cjh18242065873@163.com]

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 9. Children

Our service is not directed to children under the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction.

We may update this policy from time to time
